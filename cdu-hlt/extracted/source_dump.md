#### Testing Center Heat Load Test (HLT) Objective: 測試中⼼熱負載測試⽬標

The objective of a Testing Center Heat Load Test is to assess and verify the cooling and HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) systems' performance under different heat load conditions. It is conducted to ensure that the data center's cooling infrastructure can effectively manage the heat generated by IT equipment and maintain the desired temperature and humidity levels. The specific objectives include:

測試中⼼熱負載測試的⽬標，是在不同的熱負載條件下評估並驗證冷卻和空調系統的性能。它的⽬的是確保測試中⼼

的冷卻基礎設施，能夠有效處理IT設備產⽣的熱量，並保持所需的溫度和濕度⽔平。具體⽬標包括：

Capacity Assessment: Determine the cooling system's capacity to handle the heat generated by data center equipment at various load levels.

容量評估：確定冷卻系統在各種負載⽔平下處理測試中⼼設備產⽣的熱量的能⼒。

Redundancy Testing: Verify the performance of backup cooling systems and their ability to maintain environmental conditions during equipment failures or maintenance.

冗餘性測試：驗證備⽤冷卻系統的性能以及它們在設備故障或維護期間保持環境條件的能⼒。

Heat Distribution: Ensure even heat distribution and temperature control across the data center space.

熱分佈：確保熱量均勻分佈，並控制測試中⼼空間的溫度。

Identification of Weaknesses: Identify any weaknesses or vulnerabilities in the cooling system and make necessary improvements or adjustments.

弱點識別：確定冷卻系統中的任何弱點或漏洞，並進⾏必要的改進或調整。

After each test, the effects will be verified, systems reset and the effects verified again to ensure a return to normal operation is achieved. The tests will be recorded on the pro forma test sheets provided by HSAM, which will be included in the Document.

在每次測試之後，將驗證效應，重置系統，並再次驗證效應，以確保恢復到正常運作。測試將記錄在由HSAM提供的

標準測試表上，這些表將包括在⽂件中。

There shall be no intervention of the system operations by anyone during the testing unless a serious situation develops or during the resetting of the system as set out in the test script after completion of each failure scenario.在測試期間，除非出現嚴重情況或在完成每個故障情景後按照測試腳本的設定重置系統時，任何⼈都不得⼲預系統運

作。

## Pre-requisite Conditions 測試前提

Heat Load Test (HLT) Pre-requisite Conditions refer to the specific requirements and conditions that must be met or established before conducting these tests in a data center. These conditions are essential for ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the tests and for maintaining the integrity of the data center's infrastructure. Pre-requisite conditions for HLT should include:

熱負載測試（HLT）的前提條件是指在資料中⼼進⾏這些測試之前必須滿⾜或建立的具體要求和條件。這些條件對於

確保測試的準確性和可靠性，以及維護資料中⼼基礎設施的完整性⾄關重要。 HLT 的前提條件應該包括：

Equipment Readiness: Ensuring that all heat load, and networking devices are installed, configured, and ready for testing.

設備準備就緒：確保所有熱負載和網路設備已安裝、配置並準備好進⾏測試。

Environmental Control: Setting the data center's environmental conditions, including temperature, humidity, and airflow, to the desired operational levels.

環境控制：設置資料中⼼的環境條件，包括溫度、濕度和風流，以達到所需的運作⽔平。

Power Infrastructure: Verifying the availability of a stable power supply and confirming the proper functioning of uninterruptible power supplies (UPS).

電⼒基礎設施：確認穩定的電⼒供應可⽤性，並確認不間斷電源（UPS）的正確運作。

Network Connectivity: Ensuring that the data center's network infrastructure is operational, with redundant connectivity, if applicable.

網路連接：確保資料中⼼的網路基礎設施正常運作，如果適⽤，還需要冗餘連接。

Security and Access Control: Implementing security measures to protect the testing environment and ensuring that authorized personnel have access.

安全和存取控制：實施安全措施以保護測試環境，確保授權⼈員能夠進入。

Documentation: Preparing detailed test plans, procedures, and documentation to record the test results and observations.

⽂件化：準備詳細的測試計劃、程序和⽂件，以記錄測試結果和觀察。

Safety Measures: Establishing safety protocols and emergency response procedures in case of unexpected events during testing.

安全措施：在測試期間應對突發事件建立安全協議和應急應對程序。

Test Team Readiness: Confirming that the test team is trained, equipped, and prepared to conduct the tests.

測試團隊就緒：確認測試團隊接受培訓、配備良好並準備進⾏測試。

Communication: Coordinating with relevant stakeholders and ensuring that all parties are informed and ready for the tests.

溝通：與相關利益相關者協調，確保所有各⽅已獲通知並準備好進⾏測試。

Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring that the tests comply with industry standards, legal requirements, and any relevant regulations.

合規性：確保測試符合⾏業標準、法律要求和任何相關法規。

Test Objectives: Clearly defining the objectives, scope, and success criteria for the HLT.

測試⽬標：清晰地定義 HLT的⽬標、範圍和成功標準。

Test Schedule: Setting a schedule that minimizes disruptions to normal data center operations.

測試時間表：制定⼀個時間表，以減少對正常資料中⼼運作的⼲擾。

Risk Assessment: Identifying and mitigating potential risks and hazards associated with the tests.

風險評估：確定並減輕與測試相關的潛在風險和危害。

Change Management: Implementing change control procedures to manage any modifications made to the data center environment for testing.

變更管理：實施變更控制程序，以管理為測試對資料中⼼環境所做的任何修改。

Equipment Calibration: Calibrating test equipment and instruments to ensure accurate measurements.

設備校準：對測試設備和儀器進⾏校準，以確保準確的測量。

Meeting these pre-requisite conditions is crucial to conducting accurate and effective HLT. Failing to meet these conditions can lead to inaccurate test results, potential disruptions to data center operations, and safety risks.

滿⾜這些前提條件對於進⾏準確和有效的 HLT ⾄關重要。未能滿⾜這些條件可能導致測試結果不準確、對資料中⼼

運營的潛在⼲擾以及安全風險。


### Test environment preparation 測試環境準備

All individual systems must have commissioning test record sheets in place and reviewed by the commissioning manager, consultant engineer and/or a member of the client team.

所有獨立的系統，都必須提供測試記錄表，並由測試經理、顧問⼯程師 及/或 客⼾團隊進⾏審核。

All systems must be operating in normal conditions, with approved set points and no alarms present.

所有的系統必須在已經通過審核的設定值且沒有任何告警的情況下運轉。

All artificial heat load banks are installed within the room to simulate the maximum design heat load for the environment. Load shall be distributed within the test area to simulate IT racks. Refer to attached Rack Layout and Heat Load Plan.

所有的假負載必須安裝在機房內，以模擬最⼤的設計熱負載環境。假負載應分佈在測試區域內的機櫃中。請參閱附

件 Rack Layout 及 熱負載計畫。

Heaters are to be positioned and tested. The load requirements for each test are to be verified and balanced across the phases and sources so that on the test day the loads can be adjusted easily.

安裝假負載⾄指定位置並進⾏測試。確認每項測試的負載需求，並確認每相電源及來源端的電⼒平衡，以便在測試期

間更⽅便進⾏假負載調整。

All the equipment set points are correct and in accordance with the approved design values confirmed by the MEP consultant. (Refer to Design Parameters and Appendix on Equipment set points)

所有設備之設定值是正確的，並符合MEP顧問審核設計之設定值(請參閱附件設計參數及設備設定值附錄)

The temperature and humidity loggers are installed in suitable locations throughout the test area or at the designated location to obtain accurate information on the overall space conditions during the test.

溫濕度感測器需安裝在測試區合適或指定的位置，以取得測試過程中整體空間的準確資訊。

The data loggers are set to start recording one hour before the test with one-minute recording time interval. The data logger’s reference number shall be marked and noted on the floor plan.

數據記錄器將於測試前⼀⼩時開始記錄，紀錄時間間隔為⼀分鐘，數據記錄器記錄參考編號應於平⾯圖上標⽰並註

明。

在HLT開始前，需要安裝熱通道。

The Command Centre is set up at where the BMS workstation is located.

指揮中⼼應設置於BMS⼯作站所在地。
